Projects

Demand Navigation Plus

Alignment, Navigation | Habitat3.0, GPT-4 API, Scene Graph, Pytorch, HSSD, HM3D | Ongoing

We propose a benchmark to evaluate the ability of agents to understand human needs with hierarchical preferences and effectively explore scenarios based on those needs. We've created a dataset encompassing basic needs, preferences, and related items. Currently, we're developing an evaluation method rooted in this dataset, with plans to offer a practical implementation.

Role and Responsibilities: Jointly responsible with Dr. Wang Hongcheng, I leverage the GPT-4 API and prompt engineering to generate datasets, modify and test the Habitat 3.0 simulator, design and implement a specific model, and oversee model training and evaluation experiments.

Demand Behavior

Alignment | Omnigibson, GPT-4 API | Ongoing

This project aims to align robot behavior with human values, modeling human values through professional psychological questionnaires and using GPT-4 for data augmentation. We're developing a method to evaluate if an agent’s actions reflect human values, using the OmniGibson simulator and BDDL format for tasks.

Role and Responsibilities: As part of a six-person team, I am responsible for modifying and using part of the simulator and contributing ideas during weekly discussions.

Dex Grasp in Clutter Scene

Grasping, Manipulation | Isaac Gym, Bullet, RRT Algorithm, Gradient Field | Ongoing

Grasping items in cluttered and confined cabinet scenes is a challenging task. We aim to leverage the flexibility advantages of dexterous hands, employing the method of gradient fields to train a model capable of accomplishing this task.

Role and Responsibilities: I participated in constructing the scene and designing the data generation strategy, helping to implement the rapid generation of collision-free robotic arm poses as ground truth.

Scene-Plan GPT

LLM Planning, Scene Graph | iGibson, GPT-4 API, DETR, Scene Graph | Suspended

We proposed a novel approach that integrates a scene-graph memory with LLMs to facilitate spatial reasoning and navigation. The method incorporates RGB and Depth video embeddings into the LLM’s semantic space, enhancing visual and spatial awareness for task planning.

Role and Responsibilities: Initially aimed for CVPR 2023, we withdrew our submission due to suboptimal training results. However, I learned a lot from this project, including modifying and utilizing the iGibson simulator, fine-tuning the DETR model, and generating action list data using the GPT-4 API.
